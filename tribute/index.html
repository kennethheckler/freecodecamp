Homer Simpson

Homer Simpson is the lovable, yet innocent-minded husband and father in the longest running animated series, The Simpsons. Created by Matt Groening in 1987 and voiced by Dan Castellaneta, Homer is known for his not-to-well thought-out antics that tend to lead him and his family on some crazy adventures. His character is known for being loud, incompetent, clumsy, and short-tempered (especially to his son, Bart). He loves to eat and especially drink Duff beer. Overall, despite all of his flaws, he is devoted to his family and usually sets things right in the end.
